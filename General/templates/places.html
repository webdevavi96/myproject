Places to Explore

Ajanta and Ellora Caves

Ancient rock-cut caves of Ajanta and Ellora showcase Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain architecture with intricate carvings.

Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Enjoy the pristine beaches and tropical forests of Andaman, a serene island destination with crystal-clear waters.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Famous for its tea gardens, Darjeeling offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and colonial charm.

Hampi, Karnataka

Discover the majestic ruins of Hampi, an ancient city known for its historic temples and stunning landscapes.

Jog Falls, Karnataka

Jog Falls is one of India’s highest waterfalls, offering a dramatic sight of water plunging from a great height.

Khajuraho, Madhya Pradesh

Known for its beautifully carved temples, Khajuraho reflects India’s rich cultural and architectural legacy.

Kumarakom, Kerala

Kumarakom is a tranquil destination known for its backwaters, offering picturesque views and houseboat experiences.

Pangong Lake, Ladakh

Pangong Lake offers stunning views of clear blue waters, making it one of Ladakh's most famous travel spots.

National Park, Rajasthan

A wildlife haven, the national parks of Rajasthan are home to diverse flora and fauna in a desert landscape.

Munnar, Kerala

Munnar’s rolling hills and tea plantations make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Rameshwaram

A sacred island, Rameshwaram is famous for its temples and spiritual significance in Indian mythology.

Spiti Valley

Spiti Valley is known for its rugged mountain terrain, offering breathtaking views and adventure activities.

Udaipur, Rajasthan

Udaipur, the city of lakes, is a charming destination known for its palaces, lakes, and romantic vibes.
